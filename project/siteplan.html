BWCodeForge - Website Plan

Site Name

BWCodeForge

This name reflects the combination of Botswana (BW) and the concept of forging high-quality code, emphasizing the craftsmanship and expertise in web development.

Site Purpose

The purpose of BWCodeForge is to provide comprehensive web development services, including website design, development, maintenance, and customization. The site will showcase a portfolio of past projects to demonstrate the quality and range of services offered, and provide a contact point for potential clients.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A small business owner wants to create a professional website to showcase their products/services and attract more customers. They are looking for a web developer who can design a visually appealing and functional website within their budget.

Scenario 2: An entrepreneur has an existing website but wants to improve its performance and user experience. They are seeking a web developer who can optimize the website for speed, responsiveness, and search engine visibility.

Color Schema

Primary Color: #007bff (Blue) - Used for headings, navigation links, and call-to-action buttons.

Secondary Color: #28a745 (Green) - Used for background accents and highlighting important information.

Typography

Font: Roboto (Sans-serif) - Used for headings and body text for its modern and clean aesthetic.
