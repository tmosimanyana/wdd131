Welcome to Botswana

Botswana, a landlocked country in Southern Africa, is renowned for its political stability, multiethnic society, and rich natural resources. It is bordered by Namibia, Zambia, Zimbabwe, and South Africa.

Geography

Botswana is predominantly flat, with up to 70% of its territory being the Kalahari Desert. The Okavango Delta, one of the world's largest inland deltas, is located in the northwest and becomes a lush animal habitat during seasonal floods.

Culture

Botswana's culture is a rich blend of traditional and modern elements. The country is known for its music, dance, and crafts. The Setswana people, who form the majority, have a strong cultural heritage that influences the country's festivals and ceremonies.

Wildlife

Botswana is a wildlife paradise. It boasts numerous national parks and reserves, including the famous Chobe National Park, home to one of the largest concentrations of elephants in the world. The Moremi Game Reserve in the Okavango Delta is also notable for its biodiversity.
