About Botswana

Botswana is a landlocked country in Southern Africa known for its diverse ecosystems, including the Kalahari Desert and the Okavango Delta, which becomes a lush animal habitat during the seasonal floods.

Places to Visit

  • Chobe National Park
  • Okavango Delta
  • Makgadikgadi Pans
  • Tsodilo Hills

Culture

Botswana's culture is rich with traditional music, dance, and art. The Setswana people have a vibrant cultural heritage that is celebrated through various festivals and ceremonies.
