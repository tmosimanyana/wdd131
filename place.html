About Botswana

Botswana is a landlocked country in Southern Africa known for its diverse ecosystems, including the Kalahari Desert and the Okavango Delta, a vast inland delta that becomes a lush animal habitat during seasonal floods.

Features

  • Okavango Delta
  • Chobe National Park
  • Moremi Game Reserve
  • Kalahari Desert
  • Tsodilo Hills
  • Gaborone
