Botswana Travel Hub

Welcome to the Botswana Travel Hub, your ultimate guide to exploring Botswana!

Site Purpose

The Botswana Travel Hub is designed to provide comprehensive information about travel destinations, attractions, accommodations, and travel tips for visitors planning a trip to Botswana.

Scenarios

  • What are the top safari destinations in Botswana?
  • Where can I find reliable information about visa requirements for visiting Botswana?

Color Schema

Primary color: #007bff (used for headings and links)

Secondary color: #6c757d (used for background and accents)

Typography

Main font: Open Sans (used for body text)

Heading font: Montserrat (used for headings)
