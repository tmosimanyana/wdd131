Website Plan

Site Name: Freelance Web Development Portfolio

Reason: This name accurately reflects the purpose of the website, which is to showcase my freelance web development projects and skills.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as a portfolio showcasing my freelance web development projects, skills, and expertise. It aims to attract potential clients by demonstrating the quality and scope of previous work and providing detailed information about the services offered.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A potential client is searching for a web developer. They come across the portfolio site and want to see examples of previous projects to assess the developer's capabilities.

Scenario 2: A small business owner needs a new website. They visit the portfolio site to understand the services offered and to contact the developer for a consultation.

Color Schema

Primary Color: #007bff (Blue) - Used for headings and accents.

Secondary Color: #333 (Dark Gray) - Used for body text.

Typography

Headings: Arial, sans-serif

Body Text: Arial, sans-serif
