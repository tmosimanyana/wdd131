BWCodeForge - Website Plan

Site Name

BWCodeForge

This name reflects the combination of Botswana (BW) and the concept of forging high-quality code, emphasizing the craftsmanship and expertise in web development.

Site Purpose

The purpose of BWCodeForge is to provide comprehensive web development services, including website design, development, maintenance, and customization. The site will showcase a portfolio of past projects to demonstrate the quality and range of services offered, and provide a contact point for potential clients.

  • Showcase Portfolio: Display past projects to highlight skills and experience in web development.
  • Service Information: Provide detailed descriptions of services offered, including website design, development, maintenance, and customization.
  • Client Testimonials: Feature testimonials from past clients to build credibility and trust.
  • Blog/Resources: Share articles, tutorials, and resources related to web development to engage visitors and establish authority in the field.
  • Contact Information: Offer multiple ways for potential clients to get in touch, including a contact form and social media links.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A local bakery owner wants to expand their business by reaching more customers online. They visit BWCodeForge to find a developer who can create an appealing and functional e-commerce website.

Question: "Can you design a user-friendly e-commerce website that allows customers to browse products, place orders, and make payments securely?"

Content Needs:

  • Examples of past e-commerce projects
  • Detailed description of e-commerce development services
  • Information on payment gateway integration

Scenario 2: A tech startup needs a dynamic website that can showcase their innovative products and services. They are looking for a developer who can create a modern, responsive site that stands out.

Question: "Do you offer custom web development services that include responsive design and integration with social media platforms?"

Content Needs:

  • Portfolio showcasing responsive websites
  • Description of custom development services
  • Details on social media integration and other features

Color Schema

Primary Color: #007bff (Blue) - Used for headings, navigation links, and call-to-action buttons.

Secondary Color: #28a745 (Green) - Used for background accents and highlighting important information.

Typography

Font: Roboto (Sans-serif) - Used for headings and body text for its modern and clean aesthetic.
